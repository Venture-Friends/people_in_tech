Platform Guide

People in Tech.

Greece's premier talent discovery platform connecting tech professionals with the companies shaping the future of the Greek tech ecosystem.

Version 1.0 April 2026 Prepared for Venture Friends
Overview

What is People in Tech?

A curated discovery platform where tech companies in Greece showcase their teams, culture, and open roles — and where tech professionals build profiles, follow companies, and express interest in opportunities.

For Candidates

Discover companies, follow them, express interest in roles, and build a professional profile that companies can view.

For Companies

Claim your company page, showcase your team and tech stack, post jobs, and see which candidates are interested.

For Admins

Manage the entire platform — companies, jobs, events, claims, content, newsletters, and candidate outreach.

Candidate Experience

How It Works for Candidates

The journey from sign-up to expressing interest in a role.

1. Registration & Onboarding

Create Account

Sign up with name, email, and password. A verification email is sent — click the link to activate your account.

Onboarding Wizard — About You

Enter your professional headline, experience level (from Student to Executive), and LinkedIn URL.

Onboarding Wizard — Skills & Interests

Select up to 5 role interests, add your skills (95+ predefined + custom), pick industries and preferred locations.

Onboarding Wizard — Preferences

Choose email notification preferences: weekly digest, event announcements, and newsletter.

Start Discovering

You land on the Discover page — browse companies, follow them, and explore open roles.

The main hub for browsing the platform. Search by company name and narrow down with filters:

3. Company Profiles

Each company has a rich profile page with four tabs:

About

Company description, technologies, team size, founded year, locations, and team members.

Open Roles

Active job listings with title, location, type, and direct links to apply.

Events

Upcoming workshops, meetups, and webinars hosted by the company.

Gallery

Photos showcasing the company's office, team, and culture.

The hero section shows the company's representative (name, title, LinkedIn) for verified companies, a follow button with live follower count, and links to the company website and LinkedIn.

4. Jobs

Browse all active roles across the platform. Search by title or company, filter by job type (Remote, Hybrid, On-site), and sort by date or company name.

Job Detail Page

Each job has a dedicated page with: full description, requirements, tech stack, experience level, and an "Apply" button linking to the company's application page. Candidates can also "Express Interest" — this signals to the company that they're interested, and the company can then view the candidate's profile. Jobs can be saved to the dashboard.

Auth Gate: Anonymous visitors can preview the first 6 jobs. Signing up unlocks the full listing.

5. Events

Browse upcoming and past tech events — workshops, meetups, webinars. Filter by type, date range, and online/in-person. Save events and register directly from the platform.

6. Candidate Dashboard

A personal hub with four tabs:

Saved Companies

Companies you follow — manage from here.

Saved Jobs

Bookmarked jobs for quick access.

Alerts

New jobs and events from followed companies (30-day rolling window).

Saved Events

Events you've saved or registered for.

Company Experience

How It Works for Companies

Companies can claim their page or request a new listing.

Claiming an Existing Company

Find Your Company

Search for your company on the Discover page and visit the profile.

Click "Claim This Company"

A form asks for your name, job title, work email, and optionally your LinkedIn and a message.

Verification

If you're logged in, the claim is submitted directly. If not, a verification email is sent to your work email — click the link to confirm.

Admin Review

The platform admin reviews and approves or rejects the claim. On approval, the company becomes verified and shows your info as representative.

Multiple Claims: If a company already has a pending claim from someone else, you can still submit your own. The admin decides who to approve. A "Claim in review" label is shown alongside the claim button.

Listing a New Company

If your company isn't on the platform yet, use the "List Your Company" form. Enter the company name, website, your contact details, and role. The request goes to admin for review.

If a company with the same name already exists, you'll see a warning directing you to claim the existing page instead — preventing duplicates.

Note: Company representatives do not have direct editing access to company profiles. All company data is managed by the platform admin in communication with the representative. This ensures quality control and consistency.
Admin Guide

Platform Administration

Everything the admin can do from the admin panel at /admin.

Dashboard Overview

The admin dashboard shows key metrics at a glance:

KPI Cards

Total companies, total candidates, pending claims (includes all types), and active jobs.

Quick Actions

Pending claims alert with a direct link to the review queue. Top 5 companies by follower count.

Company Management

Full control over all company profiles from the Companies tab.

+

Add & Edit Companies

Create companies manually or edit any existing profile. The edit panel covers: name, description, industry, website, LinkedIn, careers URL, logo, cover image, size, founded year, locations, and technologies.

✨

AI Enrichment

Click the sparkle icon to auto-extract company data from their website — description, logo, LinkedIn URL, and technologies. Review the results before applying.

🔍

Job Scraping

Scan a company's careers page for open roles. Supports Greenhouse, Lever, Workable, and generic career pages. Review scraped results and selectively import them as job listings.

★

Badges & Status

Toggle Featured to highlight a company on the homepage and cards. Toggle VC Funded to show a VC-backed badge. Both are visible across the platform.

👤

Assign Representatives

In the edit panel, scroll to the "Representative" section. Enter the contact person's name, job title, work email, and LinkedIn. Saving sets the company to Verified and shows the representative on the public profile with a green badge.

Claims Queue

The claims queue surfaces three types of requests in one view:

Listing Request

Companies submitted via the "List my company" form. Shows contact info (name, email, phone, role, message). Approve to add the company to the platform. Reject to delete the record.

Company Claim

Ownership claims from logged-in users. Shows claimant details and company info. Approve to verify the company and promote the user. Reject requires a written reason.

Email Unverified

Public claims where the person hasn't clicked the email verification link yet. Dismiss to remove stale or spam entries.

Job Management

View all jobs across the platform. Toggle status between Active and Paused. Click the interest count badge on any job to see which candidates expressed interest — each links to the candidate's full profile.

Candidate Management

Search candidates by name or email. View any candidate's profile — including private profiles. The candidate's email is shown on their profile (visible to admin only) for direct outreach. Export the full candidate list to CSV.
